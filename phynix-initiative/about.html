About Us

This organisation is being built on the foundation of a peer support model

Phynix Initiative

Our way of working is to empower you in becoming in the best you want to be. you are unique, and the way we work with you should be too. We start where you are and we work towards your goal.

Vision

To become a world leader in helping people achieve their potential.

Mission

To empower individuals, groups, organisations, and communities with the knowledge necessary to increase motivation, resilience and mental wellbeing.

Values

  • Hope - We value hope,courage and perseverance, knowing that people can accomplish great things when they keep on trying
  • Client Focus - we want to walk alongside our customers, to grow with them and to create win-win solutions for both parties.
  • Integrity - We value honesty, accountability and open communication in our relationships with our clients, staff and other stakeholders.
  • Making a Difference - We are committed to action for social justice, respect for peoples’ rights and to fostering inclusion and diversity.
  • Creativity and Innovation - We value the development of new ideas and work practices that continually improve our capacity to provide excellent services.
