Hello Dear Readers


I am here, the creator of Mother Earth Macrame. I am super excited to showcase my collection of fibre art to the world of Macrame lovers. Would you call yourself a free spirit? My happy place is when I'm designing my Boho clothing, hangers and pretty little things. Though I cannot put the feeling into words, it's like turning your face to the sun after a long winter, like watching the first spring flowers bloom, like running barefoot in the woods, chasing my happiness with Mother Earth guiding me. She lifts my spirits with every knot into the cord, every brush of my fingers working their magic to make something uniquely beautiful.
