Free Contract Reviews

It is important prior to purchasing a property to have your contract reviewed so you know what you are purchasing. We provide a free and no obligation contract review service. We provide you free comprehensive written advice in simple terms, so you understand your obligations under the contract of sale.

If you do not end up proceeding with the purchase, you will have that peace of mind that we will not charge you any fees. Sounds goods? Great, fill out your details and upload your contract of sale in the form below and we will take care of the rest.

Please note that we only provide free contract reviews for any standard residential properties. This means any commercial, off the plan, unregistered or company title contracts are not included in our free contract reviews. These types of contracts are overly complex and require further investigations and review. If you want us to review these types of contract, please contact us and we can provide you with further information.
