About us

Steve Fencing Qld

Steve’s Fencing was founded on the principle that I could do a better job for a better price, and I am proud to say 32 years later this Principle is still the foundation stone of my business. Being a perfectionist by nature you can be assured that I am hands on at every job ensuring everything is built to your requirements and to industry standards. As a fully qualied QBCC licensed contractor I offer you prompt, free, no obligation quotations along with expert advice.

Services

We build timber retaining walls and all types of fencing.

All types of fencing:

Timber, Colorband, Chain wire, pool, Aluminium Slats, Dog wire, post and rail, picket, Privacy/Acoustic, Colonial, Heritage, Friendly Neighbour, Overlapped, Lattice, and rural fences.

Service Areas:

Lockyer Valley, From Leildon - Fernvale - Rosewood - ipswich and all points in between. Including Gatton, Lowood, Laidley.
