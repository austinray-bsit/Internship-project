Hi I'am Jenny Ann

I am Filipina and married to a retired swiss chef. I find it very interesting how to cook and bake, especially pastries.


My husband and I both enjoy sweets! As a hobby, I began baking doughnuts, and after posting some of my products on Facebook, people started to request orders for them, so it all began! On top of that, with the long pandemic constraints we've had for a year, this makes it possible for me to gain some money and support our large family...

The donuts are done with yeast, not fast raising powder. I know that yeast is favored by many. They're 100% natural, with real fruits and no artificial flavors.
